OpenAI response for Prompt: Using artificial intelligence what is finance 
 *************************



Finance is the study of money management and how to manage financial resources. Artificial intelligence (AI) is the ability of a computer system to learn from its environment and make decisions based on its data. AI in finance can be used to automate financial processes, identify trends in financial markets, and help financial advisors and investors make better decisions. AI can also be used to help with financial forecasting and risk management.
